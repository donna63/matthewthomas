Coaching is an ongoing relationship with regular check-ins and long-term support. Meet & Greet is a one-time intensive session focused on specific goals, assessments, and creating a personalized roadmap. Meet & Greet can be a great starting point before committing to long-term coaching.

I take a flexible, sustainable approach to nutrition. No strict diets or calorie counting unless you prefer it.
